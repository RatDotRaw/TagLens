Logseq knowledge base analysis

This project is generalized. You'll have to supply your own Logseq knowledge base and make your own conclusions.

Know yourself and go scout in your logseq files. A lot of insights can be found by getting your hands dirty by exploring and comparing the visual data with your logseq entries. It is important to take your time and jump back and forward in your logseq files and notes you'll be making.
